# Privacy Policy

**App:** Sudoku
**Last updated:** 2026-06-16

## Summary

Sudoku is an offline puzzle game. It does **not** collect, transmit, or share any
personal data. Everything the app stores stays on your device.

## Information We Collect

**None.** Sudoku does not collect personally identifiable information. We do not
have user accounts, and the app does not require an internet connection to play.

## Data Stored on Your Device

To make the game work, the app saves the following **locally on your device only**:

- Your in-progress game (board, pencil notes, timer, mistakes, undo history) so you
  can resume after closing the app.
- Your settings (selected theme, fast-mode preference, sound).
- Which puzzles you have already played, so the app avoids repeating them, and basic
  local play statistics.

This data never leaves your device. It is removed if you delete the app.

## Data We Share

We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties, because we do not
collect any.

## Third-Party Services

Sudoku does not include third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

## Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including
children.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted in this repository with a
new "Last updated" date.
